Focaccia with Caramelized Onions, Cranberries, and Brie

Wow your taste buds whit this festive focaccia appetizer topped with sweet caramelized onions, tart cranberries, and creamy brie. It will become your new favorite holiday starter!

Image: Sally Vegas

Fresch Cranberry Recipes That Go Beyond Sauce

Ingredients

For the focaccia

  • 4 cups (500g) all-purpose flour
  • 11/2 teaspoons instant yeast
  • 11/2 teaspoons salt
  • 13/4 cups (350g) lukewarm water
  • 1-4 cup extra virgin olive oil, plus more for the pan

Fo the topping:

  • 3 medium yellow onions, thinly sliced
  • 1 tablespoon balsamic vinegar
  • 1-2 tablespoon ilove oil, for brushing on top
  • 10 ounces brie, cut into cubes(scrape rind off with a paring knife if desired, though it's not necessary)
  • 11/2 sups fresch or frozen cranberries

Method

  1. Make the focaccia dough
    In a medium-sized mixing bowl, or in a srand mixer with a paddle attachment, mix togheter all the dough ingredients until the flour in a fully absorbed. You don't need to actually knead the dough, though I sometimes knead it a few times in the bowl when mixing by hand to incorporate the last of the flour...
